AI-Powered Personalized Listening

TuneIn explores AI-assisted personalization for audio lovers who care about mood, discovery, and accessibility. The concept builds a bridge between music, podcasts, and ambient sound layers tailored to each listener's context.

Our team partnered with audiophiles, neurodiverse listeners, and DJs who balance curation with surprise. We uncovered the need for lightweight controls that respect agency while celebrating serendipity.

I led product visioning and service design, aligning the experience across mobile, wearables, and voice assistants to make hands-free interaction feel intuitive.

How might we create personalized audio experiences that adapt to context without overwhelming users?
